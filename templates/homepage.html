"where words fail, music speaks."


Melodies is a new age music school that takes an all-round approach to learning music. With us, you learn the intricacies of your chosen instrument, but also how to read and write music, understand the different ways it is created and how you can manipulate notes to create your own sound.


At Melodies, our teachers comprise of full-time professional musicians who truly speak the language of music. To all of us, sound is a medium of expression, not simply a set of techniques to be learned and practiced, and we aim to help you channel yourself through it.
You can experience this in three ways - through our three month crash courses, long term classes or exam training (details here). Regardless of the type of course, student's level of knowledge or the instrument chosen, this remains our main focus and has resulted in many flourishing music artists who've developed unique styles and continued to benefit from the method at Melodies long after completing their courses.
