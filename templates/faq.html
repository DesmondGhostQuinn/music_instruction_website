Frequently Asked Questions



▲ ▼ Can I take a trial class?
Yes, we offer a free trial class to prospective students.
▲ ▼ I'm interested in joining a long term course. Is there a long term payment plan?
Yes, we offer multiple payment plans depending on the student's needs. You can contact us for more details.
▲ ▼ Can I learn multiple instruments at a time?
Due to the way our courses are structured, you cannot learn more than one instrument at a time. We focus on all-round learning, so you may find that you can easily pick up a different instrument after studying with us!
▲ ▼ I want to join the crash course. Do I need to have some prior experience with music?
Our crash courses can be taken by students of any level, even a complete beginner. The teaching style is adapted to suit your needs.
▲ ▼ Can I choose who teaches me?
Teachers take classes according to the schedule that works best. All our teachers are extremely sensitive to the varying needs of students and never take a one-size-fits-all approach. If for some reason you are insistent on learning from a specific teacher, you can find out the course schedules at the time of payment and sign up for those specific timings.
▲ ▼ Can I join a course at any time?
You can join the long term and exam prep courses at any time. The crash courses are between fixed dates, and registration must be done atleast two weeks prior (you can find more info here).
▲ ▼ Do I have to have my own instrument?
It is much preferred that you have an instrument of your own to practice with. However, for special circumstances, you can contact us and we can help you out. Please make this a last resort since practicing by yourself is very important to master an instrument.
▲ ▼ I'm a complete beginner. How good will I be at the end of the crash course?
We never, ever give students an expectation of their "level" by the end of a certain time period. Everyone learns differently and at a different pace. If you'd like to know the theory and techniques which will be covered, you can find it on the course page.
